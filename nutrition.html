Nourish from the soil up

Fresh-picked produce retains more vitamins, minerals, and protective antioxidants. Discover how our growing practices turn every harvest into a nutrition powerhouse.

Color equals phytonutrients

Each hue in your basket signals a unique blend of antioxidants that protect cells and support wellness. Mix and match colors for a complete spectrum.

Crimson + Berry

Lycopene and anthocyanins in tomatoes, beets, and berries can support heart health and recovery.

Sunrise Citrus

Oranges, squash, and sweet peppers deliver vitamin C to strengthen immunity and collagen.

Leafy Greens

Kale, bok choy, and lettuces are packed with folate, vitamin K, and magnesium for strong bones.

Indigo Night

Blueberries and purple carrots offer anthocyanins that protect eyesight and brain function.

Build a balanced farm-to-table plate

  1. Fill half with produce. Rotate raw and cooked veggies for diverse nutrients.
  2. Add quality protein. Pair with beans, pasture-raised eggs, or local tempeh.
  3. Finish with healthy fats. Drizzle cold-pressed olive oil or sprinkle toasted seeds.
